# Legal Compliance Checklist for LiTbiT-2.0

This checklist ensures your project meets legal and regulatory requirements for a web3/metaverse platform with bots and tokens.

## 1. Terms of Service (TERMS.md)

- Clearly state user rights, bot usage, NFT/token ownership, and platform rules.
- Include disclaimers for bot risks and crypto volatility.
- Reference KYC/AML, GDPR/CCPA compliance.
- Consult a blockchain lawyer to finalize.

## 2. Privacy Policy (PRIVACY.md)

- Explain what data is collected, how it's used, and user rights (access, deletion).
- List third-party services (wallets, analytics, cloud providers).
- Describe security measures.
- Ensure GDPR/CCPA compliance.
- Consult a blockchain lawyer to finalize.

## 3. Tokenomics (TOKENOMICS.md)

- Define token type (utility/governance), supply, and distribution.
- Explain token utility (bot access, NFT minting, fees).
- Describe economic model and anti-abuse measures.
- Avoid language that could classify the token as a security.

## 4. Smart Contracts & KYC/AML

- Use audited contracts for NFTs/tokens.
- Integrate KYC/AML for any financial/bot features.
- Document audit results and KYC/AML process.

## 5. Intellectual Property

- Register trademarks for LiTbiT-2.0 and unique bot algorithms.
- Add copyright/license info to your codebase.

## 6. Regulatory Research

- Research US SEC, FinCEN, and global crypto laws.
- Document findings and compliance steps.

## 7. Documentation

- Keep all legal docs (TERMS.md, PRIVACY.md, TOKENOMICS.md) up to date in your repo.
- Add a LEGAL.md summarizing compliance steps and lawyer contacts.

---

**Next Steps:**

- Review and update all legal docs with a qualified blockchain lawyer.
- Complete KYC/AML and contract audits before launch.
- Regularly review compliance as laws change.
