# LiTbiT-2.0 Vision

LiTbiT-2.0 is a web3 metaverse social media platform designed for crypto users, gamers, and social creators. It will feature:

- AI bots for trading, NFT generation, and social engagement
- Gasless transactions (e.g., via Coinbase Smart Wallet)
- NFT and token integration (Polygon, Base, Mainnet)
- Real-time social feeds and metaverse interactions
- Monetization through bots, NFTs, and subscriptions

## Success Metrics

- User retention and growth
- Bot-driven revenue
- NFT/Token trading volume
- Community engagement
